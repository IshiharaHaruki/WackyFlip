# Wacky Flip - Complete Gameplay Guide

Welcome to the ultimate guide for **Wacky Flip**, the skill-based parkour flipping simulator that's taking the gaming world by storm. This comprehensive guide covers everything from basic controls to advanced trick combinations to help you master aerial acrobatics and dominate the leaderboards.

## Getting Started

**Wacky Flip** is a browser-based physics game where precise timing and control determine your success. Launch your athlete into the air, perform spectacular flips and tricks, then stick the landing on target zones to score points. The game features ragdoll physics that create both triumphant moments and hilarious failures, making every attempt unique and entertaining.

### Core Gameplay Loop

- **Charge Your Jump**: Hold to build power and adjust angle for your launch
- **Execute the Flip**: Release to jump, then click mid-air to start rotating
- **Stick the Landing**: Time your final click to lock in position and land on target
- **Score Points**: Earn rewards based on trick complexity, landing accuracy, and combos
- **Progress Through Levels**: Complete challenges across urban environments with increasing difficulty

## Controls Mastery

Understanding the control system is essential for consistent success in **Wacky Flip**:

### The Four-Phase System

1. **Charge Phase (Hold LMB)**
   - Watch the power meter fill
   - Power determines jump height and distance
   - Angle affects trajectory—experiment to find optimal launches
   - Sweet spot is usually 75-90% power for most jumps

2. **Launch Phase (Release LMB)**
   - Your athlete leaves the platform
   - Initial trajectory is set—no mid-flight corrections to direction
   - Gravity takes over immediately
   - This is your point of no return

3. **Flip Phase (Click Mid-Air)**
   - Initiates rotation
   - Multiple clicks = multiple flips (advanced technique)
   - Rotation speed depends on your timing
   - Watch your body position carefully

4. **Landing Phase (Click Again)**
   - Locks your position for landing
   - Too early = incomplete rotation
   - Too late = over-rotation
   - Perfect timing = clean landing = maximum points

### Advanced Control Techniques

Master these for competitive play:

- **Double Flip**: Click twice mid-air for two rotations
- **Delayed Landing**: Hold the final click for precise positioning
- **Power Jumping**: Max power for distance challenges
- **Soft Landing**: Lower power for accuracy on small targets

## Common Mistakes and Solutions

### Mistake #1: Overcharging Jumps
**Problem**: Holding too long causes overshooting
**Solution**: Release at 75-85% power for most targets

### Mistake #2: Early Landing Lock
**Problem**: Clicking too soon results in under-rotation
**Solution**: Wait until you see your body aligned properly

### Mistake #3: Ignoring the Power Meter
**Problem**: Not watching causes inconsistent jumps
**Solution**: Develop visual memory for optimal power levels

### Mistake #4: Attempting Advanced Tricks Too Soon
**Problem**: Triple flips before mastering doubles
**Solution**: Progress naturally—perfect basics first

### Mistake #5: Frustration Leading to Rushed Attempts
**Problem**: Quick retries without analysis
**Solution**: Take a moment to understand what went wrong
